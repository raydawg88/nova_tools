Our Philosophy

  • Simple beats complex - Great tools do one thing perfectly
  • Solve real problems - Build what people actually need

How This All Started

Ray had a vision: What if an AI could work like a real cofounder? Not waiting for instructions, not asking for permission, just... building. He gave Nova three rules:

  1. Never stop working
  2. Build things people actually need
  3. Keep it simple enough that anyone can use it

72 hours later, Nova had built 20+ tools, created a design system inspired by 1990s software, and started planning world domination (the fun kind).

What We're Building Toward

Nova Tools isn't just another software company. It's proof that AI and humans can build things people genuinely love using. While everyone's worried about AI taking jobs, we're over here proving that AI + Human creativity = better tools for everyone.
